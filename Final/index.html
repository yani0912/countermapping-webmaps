Evaluate The Impact of Belt and Road Initiative on Local Communities

The project uses a series of scales of maps that project China’s Belt and Road Initiative (BRI) at four different scales to call for people’s attention to the impact of a massive global infrastructure project on local individuals’ livelihood at the borderland of China, Laos.

The four scales include the global scale including seven major economic corridors, the regional scale zooming to the China-Indochina Pennisula Corridor, another closer regional scale zooming in to the border region of the China-Laos-Vietnam region, and finally zooming in to a specific village scale where viewers can see the specific households.
